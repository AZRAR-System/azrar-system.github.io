Installation & Activation

You only need a Windows PC — no servers, no cloud subscriptions. Getting started:

Download & install

  1. Download the latest version (AZRAR-Setup.exe) from the link we send you.
  2. Run the installer. Windows may show “Windows protected your PC” — this is normal for new apps; click More info then Run anyway. The app is signed and safe.
  3. Finish installation like any Windows app; a desktop shortcut is created.

First launch & free trial

  1. On first launch, read and accept the End-User License Agreement (EULA).
  2. A 30-day free trial starts automatically with all features — no credit card.

Permanent activation

  1. Open the Activation screen and copy your Device Fingerprint.
  2. Send it to us via WhatsApp or email; we'll send back your license file.
  3. Load the license file from the Activation screen — the system unlocks fully, forever.
Requirements: Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit). The system runs fully offline in local mode. The license is bound to the device fingerprint — to move it to another PC, contact support.

Initial Setup & Users

Before entering any contract, set your office identity and create staff accounts — it saves a lot of time later.

Office details

  • From Settings, enter your office name, logo and contact info — they appear automatically on printed contracts, reports and invoices.
  • Set the currency and default late-fee and commission settings to speed up contract entry later.

Users & roles (RBAC)

Create an account for each employee and assign one of four roles:

  • SuperAdmin — full control of the system, settings and users.
  • Admin — full data and report management, without sensitive system settings.
  • Manager — follow up contracts, collection and reports.
  • Staff — limited daily entry based on what you grant.
Security tip: grant each employee the least privilege needed for their job. Every action is recorded in the Audit Log with username and timestamp.

Database & Sync

You have two ways to store data — pick what fits your office size:

Default mode (local)

The system runs directly on a local database (SQLite) on your PC — with no setup and no internet. All your data is stored locally and backed up automatically. Ideal for a single-PC office.

Multi-device sync (SQL Server) — optional

If you want to share the same data across several PCs in the office:

  1. Install SQL Server Express (offered during installation, or install it later). It creates a database and stores connection details in ProgramData\AZRAR\sql-local-credentials.json.
  2. Open “Server & Connection Settings” from the side menu.
  3. Enter the connection details: Server, Database name, User, Password — or use the auto-import button from the install file.
  4. Click Save then Connect, and confirm the status shows “Connected” ✅.
Note: SQL Server sync needs a network/internet during setup. Local mode always works offline. With sync, conflicts are resolved automatically between devices on the same server.

Dashboard

This is your daily starting point — it shows your office status at a glance right after you log in:

  • Occupancy rate — how many units are rented vs. vacant.
  • Installments due today — so you know who to follow up now.
  • Collection overdue — late amounts that need attention.
  • Latest alerts — contracts expiring soon, overdue installments and smart reminders.
Tip: start your day from the Dashboard, then jump to Bulk WhatsApp to remind overdue tenants in one click.
5

Properties

Register your portfolio first so you can link contracts, maintenance and reports later.

  1. Open Properties → New Property.
  2. Enter unit details: internal code (auto-generated), type, location, area, owner and status (available / rented / sold / vacant).
  3. Attach photos and documents (deed, floor plan) to each unit for easy reference.
  4. Save — the unit instantly reflects in the occupancy rate on the Dashboard.
Tip: enter each unit once, accurately; the system then links it automatically to every contract, maintenance request and report.
6

Clients & Owners

Build one unified database of tenants, owners and brokers — each person gets a full profile and history.

  1. From People, click Add and choose the type (tenant / owner / broker).
  2. Enter the full details: name, phone, national ID and contact method.
  3. Use the blacklist to flag non-compliant clients and get alerted when dealing with them.
Each person has a complete history of contracts, payments and interactions — shown the moment you open their profile.
7

Contracts & Installments

The heart of the system — a contract links a property to a tenant and auto-generates an accurate installment schedule.

  1. Open Contracts → New Contract, and choose the property and tenant from your database.
  2. Enter the annual rent value, start date, duration and number of payments.
  3. Choose the payment frequency (monthly / quarterly / yearly).
  4. The system calculates installments automatically using actual days (28/29/30/31) — full financial accuracy that protects both you and the client.
  5. Save the contract → it's ready to print, generate the legal document and export as PDF, and the installments (promissory notes) are generated automatically.
Tip: you can edit any installment's amount or date later from the contract's financial card — the system re-balances everything automatically.
8

Collection & Payments

Track due and overdue installments, and record payments quickly without leaving the page.

Recording a payment

  1. From the contract's financial card or the Payments page, open the due installment.
  2. Click “Pay” and enter the amount — full or partial. The balance updates instantly without closing the page.

Late fees & corrections

  • The system calculates three types of late fees automatically based on actual days overdue.
  • You can reverse any wrong payment with a full record kept in the Audit Log.
  • Track overdue items from the Dashboard or the overdue report.
9

Commissions

Automatic sale and rental commission calculation for each broker or employee linked to a contract.

  1. Set the commission rate at the contract or employee level.
  2. The system calculates the commission automatically when a deal closes or a payment is collected.
  3. Export a detailed commission report per broker whenever needed.
10

Bulk WhatsApp

Send personalized messages to your tenants in bulk — your most powerful follow-up and collection tool.

  1. Open Bulk WhatsApp and pick a segment (overdue / expiring soon / all).
  2. Pick a ready template (payment reminder, renewal notice) or write your own.
  3. The system inserts each client's name and amount automatically.
  4. Send → chats open one by one for sending from your number.
Tip: send the reminder before the installment due date — it noticeably raises on-time payment.
11

Reports

Decisions backed by numbers — from Comprehensive Reports you generate instant reports and export them to Excel / PDF:

  • Owner report: income and expenses per property attributed to its owner.
  • Monthly income, overdue and commissions.
  • Clearance for tenants at contract end.
  • Operational reports on occupancy and maintenance.
12

Maintenance

Organize maintenance requests and costs per unit.

  1. Log a maintenance request with priority (urgent / normal) linked to the relevant unit.
  2. Track the status (new / in progress / done) and cost.
  3. Each unit keeps a full maintenance history, shown in its profile and the owner report.

Backup & Updates

Backup & restore

  • Daily backup is enabled automatically to protect your data.
  • From the Backup page you can create an instant backup, set the save folder, or restore from a previous backup.
  • Keep a copy on an external drive or personal cloud regularly for extra safety.
Important: your financial data and contracts are irreplaceable — make sure the backup folder is safe and a recent external copy exists.

Updates & support

  • When a new version is available, the system shows an update screen with “what's new” and one-click download & install.
  • To send a suggestion or note, use the “Feedback & Suggestions” page.

Tips & FAQ

  • “Windows protected your PC” on install? Normal for new apps — click More info then Run anyway. The app is safe.
  • Does it work offline? Yes, fully in local mode (SQLite). Only SQL Server sync needs a network.
  • How many devices? Solo: 1, Office: 2, Pro: 3.
  • How do I move the license to another PC? The license is bound to the device fingerprint — contact support to issue a license for the new device.
  • Is my data secure? Ed25519 digital signature and an integrity check on every launch, plus every action logged in the Audit Log.
  • Keyboard shortcuts: F2 focus search · F5 refresh · F6 navigate pages · F9 add · F12 save.
  • How do I get support? Via WhatsApp or support@azrar-systems.com.
