Installation & Activation

Download AZRAR and install it on Windows, then activate your license or start the free trial.

  • Download the latest version (an .exe file) and install it like any Windows app.
  • On first launch, the 30-day free trial starts automatically with all features — no credit card.
  • To buy a license: copy your Device ID from the activation screen and send it to us via WhatsApp or email; we'll send your license file.
  • Load the license file from the Activation screen — the system unlocks fully.
2

Initial Setup & Users

Configure your office details and create staff accounts with permissions.

  • From Settings, enter your office name, logo and contact info (shown on contracts and reports).
  • Create users and assign each a role: SuperAdmin / Admin / Manager / Staff.
  • Set default currency, late-fee and commission settings to speed up data entry later.
3

Properties

Register your portfolio so you can link contracts and maintenance later.

  • Open Properties → New Property.
  • Enter unit details: type, location, area, owner and status (available / rented / sold).
  • Attach photos and documents (deed, floor plan) to each unit.
4

Clients & Owners

Build a database of tenants, owners and brokers.

  • From People, add the tenant/owner with full details and contact method.
  • Use the blacklist to flag non-compliant clients.
  • Each client has a full history of contracts, payments and interactions.
5

Contracts & Installments

The heart of the system — create a professional lease and get an accurate installment schedule automatically.

  • Open Contracts → New Contract, choose the property and tenant.
  • Enter rent value, start date, duration and number of payments.
  • The system calculates installments automatically using actual days (28/29/30/31) for full financial accuracy.
  • Choose payment type (monthly/quarterly/yearly).
  • Save the contract — ready to print and generate the legal document.
6

Collection & Payments

Track installment collection, partial payments and late fees.

  • From the contract card, click a due installment → Record Payment (full or partial).
  • The system supports three types of late fees, calculated automatically by days.
  • You can reverse any wrong payment with a full audit trail.
  • Track overdue items from the Dashboard or the overdue report.
7

Commissions

Automatic sale and rental commission calculation per broker or employee.

  • Set the commission rate at the contract or employee level.
  • The system calculates commission automatically when a deal closes.
  • Export a detailed commission report per broker.
8

Bulk WhatsApp

Send custom messages to all your tenants at once — your most powerful follow-up tool.

  • Open Bulk WhatsApp and pick a segment (overdue / expiring soon / all).
  • Pick a ready template (payment reminder, renewal notice) or write your own.
  • The system inserts each client's name and amount automatically.
  • Send → chats open one by one for sending.
9

Reports

Decisions backed by numbers — instant reports with one click.

  • Owner report: income and expenses per property for its owner.
  • Monthly income, overdue and commissions.
  • Clearance for tenants at contract end.
  • Export any report to Excel or PDF.
10

Maintenance

Organize maintenance requests and costs per unit.

  • Log a maintenance request with priority (urgent/normal) linked to the unit.
  • Track status (new / in progress / done) and cost.
  • Each unit has a full maintenance history.
11

Backup

Your data is local on your device — you protect it, and the system makes it easy.

  • From Settings → Backup, create a backup with one click.
  • Save backups to an external drive or personal cloud regularly.
  • Instant restore when needed — even after device failure.
12

Tips & FAQ

  • Does it work offline? Yes, fully — data is local via SQLite.
  • How many devices? Solo: 1, Office: 2, Pro: 3.
  • Is my data secure? Ed25519 signature and integrity check on every launch.
